What is Happiness?

How does it impact mental health and mood ?

"Happiness is something that people seek to find, yet what defines happines can vary from one person to the next.When most people talk about the true meaning of happiness, they might be talking about how they feel in the present moment or referring to how they feel about the life overall perspective."

Happiness is an emotional state expressed by feelings of joy, satisfaction, contentment, and fulfillment. While happiness has many different definitions, it is often described as involving positive emotions and life satisfaction. When they talk about this emotional state, it states about subjective well-being tends to focus on an individual's overall personal feelings about their life in the present.

    Two key components of happiness (or subjective well-being) are:
  • The balance of emotions: Everyone experiences both positive and negative emotions, feelings, and moods. Happiness is generally linked to experiencing more positive feelings than negative ones.
  • Life satisfaction: This relates to how satisfied you feel with different areas of your life including your relationships, work, achievements, and other things that you consider important.

Signs of Happiness

While perceptions of happiness may be different from one person to the another, there are some key signs that we look for when measuring and assessing happiness.

Some key signs of happiness include:

  • Feeling like you are living the life you wanted
  • Feeling that the conditions of your life are good
  • Enjoying positive, healthy relationships with other people
  • Feeling that you have accomplished (or will accomplish) what you want in life

Types of happiness

Happiness is derived from pleasure. It is most often associated with doing what feels good, self-care, fulfilling desires. The second one is , Happiness is derived from seeking virtue and meaning. psychologists have suggested the addition of the third component that relates to engagement.

Impact on your mood

Happiness is a sense of purpose ,health ,longevity and well-being. Happy people are more sociable and enjoy quite good social wellbeing This positive attitude translates to a variety of effects on a person's health both physically and mentally, including: Opening a person's mind to positivity, Improving a person's problem-solving ability. This allows people to learn better because they seek other positive-minded people. Positive feelings increase resilience. Resilience helps to manage stress

How to be a happier person?

Cultivate Strong Relationships

Social support is an essential part of well-being. Research has found that good social relationships are the strongest predictor of happiness.

Get Regular Exercise

Physical activity is linked to a range of physical and psychological benefits including improved mood.

Show Gratitude

keeping a gratitude list is a relatively easy, affordable, simple, and pleasant way to boost your mood.

Find a Sense of Purpose

A sense of purpose involves seeing your life as having goals, direction, and meaning. It may help improve happiness by promoting healthier behaviors.
